Website for a coin store.

Site Name:

Coinz PSWD

This name is about Coins from the Philadelpha (P), San Francisco (S), West Point (W) and Denver(D) mints.

Site Purpose:

Features of this site

  • Has current inventory for sale
  • Shows real-time silver and gold prices
  • Explanation of commemoratives
  • Contains a form to sign up for the monthly newsletter

Scenarios:

  • What can I order?
  • What does the shop want to buy?
  • How can I associate with others?
  • How do I stay up to date with trends?
  • I have some silver and gold, what is it worth?

Color Schema:

  • Paynes Gray for background colors in header and footer with white text
  • Gold color used over white for accents - must be larger fonts
  • Dark purple Used for form and light blue background

Typography

  • EB+Garamond - used for headers
  • Mulish - used for form text
  • Noto+Serif - Used for nav
  • Roboto - used for general text
