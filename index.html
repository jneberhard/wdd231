Provo, UT

The city of Provo is the 4th largest city in the state of Utah. Is is the county seat of Utah County. It is about 45 miles south of Salt Lake City. To the West of the city is a fresh water lake named "Utah Lake". The motto of Provo is "Welcome Home". During the winter there is some snow fall, but usually not more than a few inches at a time. The summer temperatures are warm, sometimes reaching into the high 90's and above. About 80% of the population is LDS with about 50-60 percent of those actively attending meetings. Brigham Young University is located in Provo. Notable businesses in Provo include Nu Skin, Qualtrics, and Utah Valley Regional Medical Center. There are many events held in Provo that bring people to gather together including the Freedom Festival around July 4, BYU Football games, various conventions, and during the 2002 Olympics, Women's Ice Hockey at the Peaks Arena.
